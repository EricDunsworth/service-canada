My Service Canada Account

Pension benefits and services in MSCA

On this page

  • Pension benefits you can apply for in MSCA
  • If you have already applied in MSCA
  • Update your personal information
  • Submit and view your documents online
  • Get your CPP/OAS tax slip online

To access these services in MSCA, you must register or sign in to MSCA and go to the Canada Pension Plan/Old Age Security (CPP/OAS) page.

Pension benefits you can apply for in MSCA

Long term benefits for severe and prolonged disability

  • CPP disability benefits

Pension benefits when someone dies

  • CPP death benefit
  • CPP survivor’s pension
  • GIS - Allowance for the Survivor
  • CPP children's benefit ages 18 to 25

Benefit support for spouses or former spouses

  • CPP pension sharing
  • CPP credit split

Retirement beneits based on earning, CPP contributions and income

  • CPP retirement pension
  • Old Age Security (OAS) pension

Additional benefits for low income seniors

  • Guaranteed Income Supplement (GIS)
  • GIS - Allowance for people aged 60 to 64

If you have already applied in MSCA

View application status

Select "Application status" to view messages about your CPP, CPP disability benefits or OAS application status.

View payment information

Select "Payment information" to view payment details. You can print an official proof of payment report of your CPP, CPP disability benefits and OAS benefits. You will be able to view your payment information for the current and last 2 years.

Note: OAS recipients will only be able to view their most recent payment. Contact OAS for a copy of your past payment information.

View estimated CPP benefits

If you have contributed to the CPP, you can view your estimated monthly CPP benefits by selecting "Estimated monthly CPP benefits."

Request child-rearing provision

If you have applied or are receiving a CPP retirement pension or CPP disability benefits, you may request the child-rearing provision by selecting "Request my child-rearing provision."

Delay receiving the OAS/GIS pension after receiving a notification letter of automatic enrolment

You can delay your OAS/GIS pension by selecting "Delay receiving my OAS pension" and follow the directions.

Update your personal information

Banking information

To change your banking information and select the date you want the change to take effect, select "Direct deposit."
Once you have made the change and received confirmation, you may use the link at the bottom of the page to view your changes right away.

You can share your direct deposit banking information between the CPP and the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

Note: It can take up to 30 days for changes to your banking information to take effect. To ensure your next payment is sent to your new account, give us your new banking information at least 30 days before your payment date.

It is not possible to change the information for foreign direct deposit online. To change your direct deposit, contact us to speak with one of our agents over the phone.

Address

To change your address and select the effective date, select "Address."

Once you made the change and received confirmation, you may use the link at the bottom of the page to view your changes right away.

Note: It is not possible to change the information for foreign addresses online. To change your address, contact us to speak with one of our agents over the phone.

Telephone number

To view, change or delete your home, work, and mobile telephone numbers, select "Telephone number."

Name, marital status or date of birth

You can’t make this type of update on MSCA. Contact us by phone to speak with one of our agents. They can tell you how to update this information.

Give consent for someone to communication on your behalf

To view, add, change and/or delete an authorized person who has your consent to communicate with Service Canada about your CPP, CPP disability or OAS account, select "Give consent for someone to communicate with CPP/OAS on my behalf."

Submit and view your documents online

Submit supporting documents for CPP disability

You can upload supporting documents for a CPP disability application by selecting "Upload documents."

This service is only available for:

  • Disability pension
  • Post-retirement disability benefit
  • Request for reconsideration for CPPD
Submit a declaration of attendance at school or university

Students aged 18 to 25 who are renewing their children’s benefit are now able to submit their proof of enrolment by signing into or registering for MSCA.

To submit your request online:

  • sign in or register to MSCA
  • navigate to the Canada Pension Plan / Old Age Security section
  • select "Submit Declaration of attendance at school or university"
Submit a request for reconsideration of a decision

If you disagree with a decision in relation to your application for a CPP, CPP disability or OAS benefit, you may ask us to reconsider our decision.

To submit your request online:

  • sign in or register to your MSCA
  • navigate to the Canada Pension Plan / Old Age Security section
  • select "Request for reconsideration"
View your CPP Statement of Contributions

The online printed version of your Statement of Contributions is considered a legal document. You may request to have your Statement of Contributions mailed to you by selecting "CPP contributions" or by contacting CPP.

Get your CPP/OAS tax slip online

Tax slips

If you received CPP/OAS benefits in the last 6 years, you can view and print your tax slips (T4A(P), T4A(OAS) and NR4) by selecting "Tax slips." Your online tax slip is an official document. You can attach it to your income tax return.

Tax slip mailing options

You can choose to receive your tax slips electronically or by mail by selecting "Tax slip mailing options."

Federal voluntary tax deductions

To view, start, change or stop a federal voluntary tax deduction, select "Federal voluntary tax deductions for CPP/OAS."

Once you have completed the change, you will be able to view your:

  • estimated gross amount
  • federal voluntary tax deduction
  • estimated net monthly payment after deduction
