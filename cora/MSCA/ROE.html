Access record of employment on the web

COVID-19

Due to the coronavirus disease (COVID-19) outbreak, there are changes to this program.

  • Consult the changes for employers – Record of employment (ROE)

Record of employment on the web (ROE Web) is a secure portal that allows you to create and submit records of employment (ROEs) online for your business, instead of completing paper forms.

Sign in

If you already have a ROE Web account, choose an option to sign in:

  • Sign in with GCKey
  • Sign in with your bank
Forgot your sign in information?

If you forgot your GCKey sign-in information, select “Sign in with GCKey” and follow the instructions to recover your information on the Welcome to GCKey page.

If you forgot your online banking sing-in information, you must recover it with your bank before you can access your ROE Web account.

If you have new GCKey or bank sign in information, you will need to recover access to your ROE Web account. Sign in with GCKey or your bank with your new information and follow the instructions to recover your ROE Web account on the “Create my profile” page. You will need to provide your ROE Web User Reference Number (URN) and to answer your security questions.

Register

If you're a new user and don't have an ROE Web account, choose an option to register:

  • Register with GCKey

    GCKey is a unique electronic credential issued by the Government of Canada. It lets you securely access ROE Web and multiple other Government online services.

    What is GCKey

    Do you have a GCKey already?

    • Yes, I need to sign in
    • No, I need to create one
  • Register with your bank

Sign in to GCKey to access ROE Web

Once you have successfully signed in to GCKey, you will automatically be redirected to create your ROE Web account.

Start with GCKey

Create a GCKey to access ROE Web

You must sign up for a GCKey before you can register to ROE Web. Select “Start with GCKey” below and follow the sign-up instructions on the Welcome to GCKey page.

Once you have successfully signed up to GCKey, you will automatically be redirected to create your ROE Web account.

Start with GCKey

  1. Verify that your bank is a Sign-in Partner

    To register to ROE Web with your bank, you must have a Canadian online bank account with one of our Sign-In Partners.

    If your bank is not listed as a Sign-in Partner you must register to ROE Web using GCKey.

    Once you have successfully signed in with your bank, you will automatically be redirected to create your ROE Web account  

    Start with your bank

    Note: You will temporarily leave the government of Canada website to use your Sign-in Partner. This is a secure site. Your financial or banking information will not be shared with Employment and Social Development Canada and Service Canada. Your bank or credit union will not know which government service you are using.
