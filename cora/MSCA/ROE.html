Access Record of employment on the web

COVID-19

Due to the coronavirus disease (COVID-19) outbreak, there are changes to this program.

  • Consult the changes for employers – Record of employment (ROE)

Record of employment on the web (ROE Web) is a secure application that enables you to create and submit records of employment (ROEs) online for your business, rather than completing paper forms. It is secure, flexible and improves the quality and accuracy of your ROEs.

Sign in

If you already have an access code or a provincial digital ID, choose an option to access MSCA:

  • Sign in with GCKey
  • Sign in with your bank
If you need to recover your account

In order to recover access to your ROE Web account, you will need your User Reference Number. You will also be required to answer your security questions.

  1. Select your sign in preference: Sign-In Partner or GCKey
    • If you are using a Sign-In Partner, select your financial institution and enter your banking credentials
    • If you choose to create a new GCKey, click the Continue to GCKey button. On the Welcome to GCKey page, click on the Sign Up button on the right hand side of the screen. Be sure to record your new User ID and password and keep it in a safe place
  2. Once you have agreed to the Privacy Statement and Terms of use for Accessing ROE Web,
    • You will be directed to a page that offers the opportunity to register as a new user or recover access to an existing profile.
    • Select Option 2: “Recover access to my existing record of employment on the Web profile by providing my User Reference Number and answering my security questions” or click on the Find my User Reference Number link
  3. If you selected Option 2:
    • Enter your User Reference Number and click Continue
    • Answer your three security questions and click Continue
    • On the confirmation page, click Continue to start using your new user name and password to access your ROE Web account
  4. If you click on the link Find my User Reference Number
    • Enter your Associated Email Address and click Continue
    • Answer your three security questions and click on Continue
    • On the confirmation page, click Continue to start using your new user name and password to access your ROE Web account

New user

If you do not have an account, choose an option to register:

  • Register with GCKey
  • Register with your bank

  1. Sign in or sign up with GCKey

    • If you already have a GCKey, select “Start with GCKey” below then select “Sign In” on the left side of the Welcome to GCKey page and follow the instructions  
    • If you don’t have a GCKey, select “Start with GCKey” below then select “Sign Up” on the right side of the Welcome to GCKey page and follow the instructions
    • If you have a GCKey but forgot your username or password, you will need to create a new GCKey. Select “Start with GCKey” below then select “Sign Up” on the left side of the Welcome to GCKey page and follow the instructions

  2. Create your ROE Web account

    Once you have signed in with GCKey, you will automatically be directed to create your ROE Web account.

  1. Verify that your bank is a Sign-in Partner

    • Verify the list of sign-in partners 
    • If your bank is listed as a Sign-in Partner, select "Start with my bank" below 
      • You will temporarily leave the government of Canada website to access the Government Sign-in by Verified.me page
      • On the Government Sign-in by Verified.me page select your bank (Sign-in Partner) and enter your online banking sign-in information 
    • If your bank is not listed as a Sign-in Partner, you must register to ROE Web using GCKey. 

  2. Create your ROE Web account

    • Once you sign in with your bank, you will automatically be directed to create your ROE Web account  

    Please note: None of your financial or banking information will be shared with Employment and Social Development Canada and Service Canada. Your bank or credit union will not know which government service you are using.
