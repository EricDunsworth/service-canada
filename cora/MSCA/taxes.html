How to access your tax slips in MSCA

To access your tax slips in My Service Canada Account (MSCA), you must register or sign in to MSCA.

Once registered or signed in, select the “Employment Insurance” or "Canada Pension Plan/Old Age Security" button and scroll down to the “Tax information” section.

You can also access Canada Revenue Agency (CRA)'s My Account for Individuals directly from MSCA. Once signed in, select the “Access CRA” button.

Tax slips available in MSCA

You can request, view, save or print official copies of tax slips in MSCA.

Access tax slips that are available online

You can safely and securely view, save or print official copies of the following tax slips in MSCA:

  • T4E for Employment Insurance (EI)
  • T4A/NR4 for Canada Pension Plan (CPP) and Old Age Security (OAS)

You can access your tax slips for the current year and the past 6 years.

A self-printed copy of your electronic tax slip is considered an official document by the Canada Revenue Agency. It can be attached to your income tax return.

Amended tax slips

You can use MSCA to view, save or print amended versions of your EI and CPP tax slips if they have been changed since they were originally issued.

Amended OAS tax slips are not available in MSCA. They will be mailed to you.

Request tax slips that are only available by mail

Your tax slips will be mailed to you if:

  • your OAS tax slip is changed
  • you received an overpayment and will have a tax slip with a negative amount
  • you received OAS or CPP benefits, resulting from one of our social security agreements with another country, and meet any of these conditions:
    • your CPP payment consists of both CPP and Régime de rentes du Québec
    • you have a negative benefit amount: you received an overpayment amount greater than the year-to-date benefit amount paid during the tax year
    • you live in Quebec and receive CPP benefits
    • you received an overpayment from another government program, for example the CRA or Veterans Affairs
  • you received the One-Time Payment for Older Seniors
  • you have not enrolled to receive your CPP and OAS tax slips online

How to change your tax slip mailing option

You can change your mailing option at any time by signing in to MSCA and selecting “Tax slip mailing options.” You can choose between “Mail” and “Online.”

If you change your mailing option to “Mail,” you must do so by the first week of January of each year. If not, the change will take effect the following year.

Your tax slips will always be available online in MSCA, even if you choose to receive them by mail.

Service standards to receive tax slips in MSCA

Tax slips

Online tax slips are available in MSCA beginning February 1 of each year. You will not be notified when they are available and will not receive them by mail.

Amended tax slips
  • Amended CPP tax slips are available online 7 to 10 days after the amendment is processed
  • Amended EI tax slips are available online 4 to 6 weeks after the amendment is processed

Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) resources

You can submit your tax slips with your income tax return to the CRA. To learn more on how to file your income tax return online, visit Get ready to do your taxes.
