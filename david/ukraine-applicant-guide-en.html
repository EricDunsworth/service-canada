Ukraine immigration measures: Financial assistance

On this page

  • Applicant information
  • Application for adult dependant adult or minor
  • Guardian application for unaccompanied minor
  • Direct deposit information

Before you apply, please ensure that you have your in-Canada bank account information from a Canadian financial institution and a copy of the IMM1442 document for yourself and any dependant(s) or minor (s) that you are applying for.

When completing the application, ensure the information matches exactly as it appears on the IMM1442 document provided by Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC).

Attestation – Must be answered in order to continue with the rest of the application

Note

All fields marked with an Asterisk (*) must be complete

Applicant information

Last name*

Last name as it appears on your IMM1442, no Cyrillic script can be accepted.

First name*

First name as it appears on your IMM1442, no Cyrillic script can be accepted.

Unique Client Identifier*

Unique Client Identifier is a number with 8-10 digits, assigned to you by IRCC, please see Appendix A sample document where it can be found.

IMM1442 document number*

IMM1442 document number, provided by IRCC, one alpha followed by nine numbers, please see Appendix A sample document where it can be found.

Date of birth*

Must be complete (year/month/day), applicants will not be acceptable with missing dates or dates replaced with asterisk(s)“*”.

Email address

Used to send confirmation page when applying online.

Phone number

May be used by Service Canada agents to speak to applicants.

Mailing address*

Mailing address must be in Canada.

Application for dependant adult or minor

This section needs to be completed when applying for a dependant adult or minor. If you require more space, request another application or write on the back on the form. Please ensure all information is provided.

Last name of dependent*

Last name of dependent as it appears on their IMM1442, no Cyrillic script can be accepted.

First name of dependent*

First name of dependent as it appears on their IMM1442, no Cyrillic script can be accepted.

Date of birth of dependent*

Must be complete, (year/month/day), applicants will not be acceptable with missing dates or dates replaced with asterisks “*”.

Unique Client Identifier*

Unique Client Identifier is a number with 8-10 digits, please see Appendix A sample document where it can be found.

IMM1442 document number*

IMM1442 document number, one letter followed by nine numbers. Please see Appendix A sample document where it can be found.

Guardian application for unaccompanied minor

Proof of Guardianship and a copy of the minor’s IMM1442 document must be submitted at the time of application. A separate application must be submitted for each minor.

Guardian’s last name*

Guardian’s first name*

Mailing address*

Address where minor will be staying (must be in Canada).

Phone number

Email address

Confirmation of Guardianship*

  • If a minor is travelling alone, they require a letter of authorization signed by both parents or by their legal guardian. The letter should be in English or French and include:
    • The parents (or legal guardians) address and telephone number, and
    • The name, address and telephone number of the adult who will be legally responsible for the child in Canada.
  • *IMM1442 Travel document for minor

Last name of minor*

As it appears on the IMM1442, no Cyrillic script can be accepted.

First name of minor *

As it appears on the IMM1442, no Cyrillic script can be accepted.

Date of birth of minor*

Must be complete (year/month/day), applicants will not be acceptable with missing dates or dates replaced with “*” If *’s is used for their Date of Birth, please contact 1-833-760-1162, Hours of operations: 8:00 to 20:00PM EST.

Unique Client Identifier*

A number with 8-10 digits, please see Appendix A sample document where it can be found.

IMM1442 Travel document number*

One letter followed by 9 numbers, please see Appendix A sample document where it can be found.

Direct deposit information

Sample of Direct Deposit Guidelines

Institution Number: 3 digits

Transit (Branch) Number: 5 digits

Account Number: 7 to 12 digits

Name: The account holder must bear the name of the individual submitting the application

Appendix A

Sample of IMM1442 Travel Document

Note

The IMM1442 document is issued to all temporary residents and refugee claimants authorized to enter and remain in Canada.

The content of the IMM1442 depends on the resident’s status (study, work, visitor, refugee, temporary resident).
