Canadian passports and travel documents

Apply for a passport, processing times, service locations, new requirements and what to do if your passport is lost, stolen or damaged.

When do you need your passport

2 business days or less

  • Go to a passport office with urgent pick-up service
  • No appointment, walk-in only
  • Proof of travel or need required
  • Extra fees apply

3 to 25 business days

  • Apply at a passport office
  • By appointment or walk-in
  • Proof of travel or need required
  • Extra fees apply (3 to 9 business days)

26 business days or more

  • Apply at any Service Canada Centre
  • By appointment, walk-in, or mail
