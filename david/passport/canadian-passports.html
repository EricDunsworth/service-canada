Canadian passports

We have new measures to help manage wait times at passport offices in Toronto, the Greater Toronto Area (GTA), Montréal, Vancouver, Calgary and Edmonton. We’ll prioritize those who need a passport within 48 hours. We’ll make arrangements for people with longer term travel plans to be served at other locations.

Urgent passport requests

If you need a passport within the next 2 business days, please visit a specialized passport site that offers urgent pick-up service. Proof of travel or need is required.

Travelling within the next 45 business days

You must apply in person at a one of the 35 passport sites. A proof of travel or need is required.

Appointments are recommended as walk-in service is offered only if capacity allows. Please book your appointment online at eServiceCanada.

Note: It may take up to 2 weeks, plus mailing time. Some offices offer urgent and/or express and regular pick-up services.

You must also apply at one of these offices if:

  • only 1 parent of a child signs the application form unless:
    • there is only 1 parent listed on the child’s birth certificate or proof of parentage document
    • you submit a death certificate for the other parent

Travelling beyond the next 45 business days

You can apply:

  • in person at your local Service Canada Centre. No proof of travel or need required
  • by mail

Appointments are recommended as walk-in service is offered only if capacity allows. Please book your appointment online at eServiceCanada.

Note: It may take up to 9 weeks plus mailing time if you apply by mail or at a Service Canada Centre.

Booking an appointment online through eServiceCanada

In accessing eServiceCanada from your computer, tablet or cellphone, you can book your appointment online 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Processing times

For a passport application submitted:

  • in person at passport site (Service Canada Centre – Passport Service): It may take up to 2 weeks, plus mailing time
  • in person at a Service Canada Centre or by mail: It may take up to 9 weeks, plus mailing time

Mailing times vary across the country.

Payment information

You can pay your application fees by:

  • certified cheque or money order (postal or bank) made payable to the Receiver General for Canada
  • credit card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • debit card

Forms

Application forms are available on the web page Find a passport or travel document form.

If you meet certain criteria, you can renew your passport using the Simplified Renewal Application Process.

If you submitted your application more than 20 business days ago

Submit a status request through this secure online form.

Note: Information regarding a submitted passport application will only be released to the applicant.
