When do you need a passport

The process to follow depends on when you need the passport for.

  • Less than 2 days

    If you need a passport within the next 2 business days.

  • Less than 25 days

    If you need a passport within the next 25 business days

  • 25 days or more

    If you need a passport beyond the next 25 business days

Urgent passport requests

If you need a passport within the next 2 business days:

  1. Find the application form for you.

    • Renewing your passport (if lost, stolen, damaged or expired)

      You can renew your passport if:

      • you were 16 or older on the date you applied
      • your passport was valid for 5 to 10 years
      • your passport was issued within 15 years of the expiry date on it, and
      • your new passport will have the same name, date of birth, place of birth and gender identifier as your old passport
      How to renew your passport
    • New adult passport application

      To apply for a Canadian passport, you must be:

      • a Canadian citizen, and
      • 16 years of age or older
      How to apply for a passport
    • Child’s passport (under the age of 16)

      You can only apply for a child’s passport if:

      • one of the child’s parents (if you’re not separated or divorced)
      • the parent who has custody of the child (if you are separated or divorced) with the correct documentation
      • the child’s legal guardian, with the correct documentation
      How to apply for a child passport

  2. Apply at the right office

    • Bring the completed application form in person to a specialized passport site that offers urgent pick-up service
    • A proof of travel or need is required
    • Do not book an appointment

Travelling within the next 25 business days

    3. Processing requires up to 10 business days, plus mailing time. Some offices offer urgent and/or express and pick-up service.

Travelling beyond the next 25 business days

If you need a passport beyond the next 25 business days:

      • the parent who has custody of the child (if you are separated or divorced) with the correct documentation
      • the child’s legal guardian, with the correct documentation

  2. Apply at the right office

    • Apply by mail or in person at your local Service Canada Centre.
    • Book your appointment online at eServiceCanada - Appointments are recommended as walk-in service is offered only if capacity allows
    • No proof of travel or need is required

    3. Processing requires 20 business days, plus mailing time.

Appointments and specialized passport sites

  • Appointments are generally recommended
  • If you can't get an appointment in time, you can walk in to one of the specialized passport sites (limited capacity)

Booking an appointment

Access eServiceCanada from your computer, tablet or cellphone, to book your appointment online 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
