Instructions - Ukraine immigration measures: Financial assistance

SessionManager.cs

The Government of Canada has committed to providing direct one-time transitional financial support for individuals fleeing Ukraine and arriving under the Canada-Ukraine Authorization for Emergency Travel (CUAET). Payments will be made through Service Canada, with information provided by the applicants and IRCC.

Before you start

Technical instructions

  1. All fields with an asterisk (*) are mandatory and must be completed.
  2. Use the buttons at the bottom of the page rather than the browser buttons at the top when navigating within this questionnaire. Selecting a link on the menu bar at the top will close your session, and any information that you entered will be lost.
  3. After a 60-minute period of inactivity, you will receive a notice that your session has expired; to continue your session and avoid losing information, you must click the "OK" button within 5 minutes.
  4. If you do not complete the questionnaire in one session, any answers entered during this session will be lost and you will have to start over.

Applying for yourself and or immediate family

Before you apply, please ensure that you have in hand:

  • your account information from a Canadian financial institution
  • a copy of the IMM1442 Travel document for yourself and any dependant(s) or minor(s) that you are applying for

Guardians

If you are an ineligible guardian applying on behalf minors, you will need all of the following:

  • your banking information
  • a proof of guardianship
  • the IMM1442 for the eligible recipient

Start the application process

Are you applying for yourself and or dependant(s) or as a guardian for a minor?
Attestation for all application types

Important: Please wait a minimum of 24 hours after you have received your IMM1442 to submit an application

Please ensure to enter information as it appears on your or your dependants IMM1442 document. If *’s is used for your date of birth, please contact the Ukraine immigration measures for financial assistance information line at 1-833-760-1162. Hours of operations: 8:00 to 20:00 EDT.

A representative from Service Canada may contact you in order to request that you visit the nearest Service Canada Centre.
