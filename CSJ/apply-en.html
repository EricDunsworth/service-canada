Canada Summer Jobs applicant guide

Apply

From: Employment and Social Development Canada

The application deadline is January 12, 2023 at 11:59 a.m. Pacific Standard Time.

Applications received or postmarked after the deadline date will not be considered.

Apply using the Grants and Contributions Online Services (GCOS) Recommended

What you can do if you apply through GCOS:

  • Save a partially filled out application online to complete at a later date
  • Submit your application online
  • Submit supporting documents
  • Review submitted applications
  • Track your application status
  • Sign agreements
  • Manage active projects
  • Review past projects submitted through GCOS
Confirmation of receipt

Once a GCOS application has been successfully submitted, a 12-character confirmation number will be immediately generated by the system. This confirmation is considered the acknowledgement of receipt of your application. You will also receive a confirmation of receipt sent by email. If you do not receive a confirmation number acknowledging the application has been received, your application has not been submitted. Please ensure to click "submit" before you exit your application. For more information on the GCOS application confirmation number, please consult the Grants and contributions online services: Canada Summer Jobs user guide.

Moreover, if you experience any technical issues, please refer to the Grants and contributions online services: Canada Summer Jobs user guide.

Other ways to apply

Please note that the ability to submit an application in person or by mail could be impacted by office closures.

Applications received by fax or by email will not be considered.

Alternative online application

What you can do if you apply through the alternative online application:

  • Save a partially filled out application online to complete within 48 hours
  • Submit your application online
  • Submit supporting documents

What you can not do if you apply through the alternative online application:

  • Review submitted applications
  • Track your application status
  • Sign agreements
  • Manage active projects
  • Review past projects submitted through GCOS

Before you apply

  • Applications are erased if left inactive for 20 minutes or more.
  • Application drafts can be saved for up to 48 hours. You must provide your email address and create a password. Once saved, an email will be sent to you with a link to resume completion of the application.
  • Resume your application: To access your saved application within 48 hours of starting it, click the link in the email we sent you. After 48 hours, your saved application will be lost.
  • Lost password: If you forget your password, it cannot be reset. You'll need to re-start your application.
  • Print application before submitting it: To print a copy of your online application for your records, you must print a copy before submitting your application online. Once submitted, you will not be able to print it.
  • You must still click “submit” to complete your application. If you do not click "submit", your application will not be received and processed.

Confirmation of receipt

Once a fillable application has been successfully submitted, a 9-digit confirmation number is immediately generated by the system. This confirmation is considered the acknowledgement of receipt of your application. You will also receive a confirmation of receipt sent by email. If you do not receive a confirmation number acknowledging the application has been received, you have not completed the submission properly.

In person

What you can do if you apply in person:

  • Submit your application in person
  • Submit supporting documents in person

What you can not do if you apply in person:

  • Save a partially filled out application online to complete at a later date
  • Submit your application online
  • Submit supporting documents
  • Review submitted applications
  • Track your application status
  • Sign agreements
  • Manage active projects
  • Review past projects submitted through GCOS

Apply now

Your application must be received before the close of business of the Service Canada Office on January 12, 2023.

Consult the hours of service of the Service Canada Offices. Please note additional administrative delays might apply.

By mail

What you can do if you apply by mail:

  • Submit your application by mail
  • Submit supporting documents by mail

What you can not do if you apply by mail:

  • Save a partially filled out application online to complete at a later date
  • Submit your application online
  • Submit supporting documents
  • Review submitted applications
  • Track your application status
  • Sign agreements
  • Manage active projects
  • Review past projects submitted through GCOS

Apply now

Your application must be postmarked on or before January 12, 2023. Consult the CSJ website for the address where to send your application. Additional administrative delays might apply.

Confirmation of receipt

An acknowledgment confirming receipt will be emailed to you within 14 calendar days. Retain this confirmation, as you will need it for future communication with Service Canada.

Submit only one copy of your application

Submit only one copy of your application to Service Canada. Submitting multiple copies of the same application or submitting the same application by using various methods will cause longer processing times for your application.
